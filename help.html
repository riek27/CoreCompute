Help Center

Everything you need to get started with HyperAstraAI

Welcome to HyperAstraAI! Start by creating your account to access our platform simulation. You can explore all features in demo mode before committing.

Our platform integrates seamlessly with popular ML frameworks including TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Hugging Face. You can test compatibility and performance with your existing workflows without any setup required.

The demo environment provides a complete simulation of our distributed computing infrastructure, allowing you to evaluate performance and compatibility risk-free.

Our simulation interface allows you to configure and test distributed computing jobs without actual GPU allocation. Start by selecting your preferred framework and model type.

Choose from available GPU tiers to match your performance requirements. The simulation accurately mirrors real distributed compute scheduling, giving you realistic performance expectations.

Monitor job progress in real-time and adjust parameters as needed. All simulations use credit-based allocation, so you can experiment without financial commitment.

HyperAstraAI uses a credit-based system representing GPU compute usage. In simulation mode, credits help you estimate real-world costs without actual charges.

Each credit corresponds to specific compute resources and time allocations. Monitor your credit usage through the dashboard to optimize your resource allocation.

Future production versions will include detailed spending analytics and cost prediction tools to help you budget effectively for large-scale ML training workloads.

GPU owners and data centers can connect idle resources to the HyperAstra network. Monetize your underutilized hardware while supporting the AI research community.

Future onboarding will be available via API or lightweight node agent installation. The process is designed for minimal operational overhead with maximum security.

Providers receive competitive compensation based on GPU tier, utilization, and market demand. Join our waiting list to be notified when provider registration opens.

Your HyperAstraAI account provides access to all platform features. Production accounts will include comprehensive usage analytics, credit management, and team collaboration tools.

Secure authentication ensures your models and data remain protected. Future releases will include API access for programmatic platform interaction.

Account settings allow you to manage notifications, billing preferences, and access permissions as your usage scales.

For technical support and platform assistance, contact our team at support@hyperastra.ai.

Our global support team typically responds within 24 hours. For urgent issues during simulation or testing, please include your session ID and detailed description of the problem.

We welcome feedback and suggestions to improve the HyperAstraAI platform. Let us know how we can better serve your distributed computing needs.
