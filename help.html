Frequently Asked Questions

How does HyperAstraAI compare to traditional cloud providers?

HyperAstraAI connects you to a global network of idle GPUs, offering up to 70% cost savings compared to traditional cloud providers. Our decentralized approach eliminates the overhead of maintaining large data centers, passing the savings directly to you while providing comparable or better performance for AI workloads.

What AI frameworks and models are supported?

We support all major AI frameworks including TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Hugging Face Transformers. Our platform is compatible with most popular model architectures for text generation, image classification, object detection, reinforcement learning, and custom models. Check our documentation for the complete list of supported frameworks and version compatibility.

How is data security and privacy handled?

Security is our top priority. All data transfers are encrypted end-to-end, and we implement strict isolation between jobs. You maintain full ownership of your models and data. We never store your training data permanently, and all temporary data is automatically wiped after job completion. For enterprise customers, we offer additional security features and compliance certifications.

How does the credits system work?

Credits are our platform's currency for accessing GPU resources. One credit typically represents one hour of computation on an entry-level GPU. Higher-tier GPUs consume credits at a faster rate. You can purchase credit packages that never expire, and we offer volume discounts for larger purchases. Our pricing calculator helps estimate credit requirements for your specific workloads.

What happens if a GPU node fails during my job?

Our platform automatically handles node failures by checkpointing your training progress and migrating to a healthy node. You don't lose progress or pay for incomplete work. For critical workloads, we recommend enabling automatic checkpointing at regular intervals to minimize potential recomputation.

When will HyperAstraAI be available to the public?

We're currently in private beta with select partners and early access users. Public availability is planned for Q2 2025. Join our waitlist to get early access and be notified when we open to the public. Enterprise customers can contact us directly for dedicated deployment options.
