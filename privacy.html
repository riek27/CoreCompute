Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2025

At HyperAstraAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized AI compute platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for our services, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company details
  • Billing Information: Payment details, billing address
  • Profile Information: Job title, preferences, communication preferences

Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Platform interactions, feature usage, session duration
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Log Data: Error reports, performance metrics, system events

AI Workload Data

For compute jobs, we process:

  • Model Data: AI models, training scripts, configuration files
  • Dataset Information: Metadata about training datasets
  • Job Metrics: Performance data, resource utilization, results

Important: We never permanently store your training data or models. All compute data is encrypted and automatically deleted after job completion.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our decentralized AI compute services
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Communicate important updates and security notices
  • Ensure compliance with our terms and applicable laws
  • Develop new features and services

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

Third Party Purpose Data Shared
Payment Processors Transaction processing Billing information, payment details
Cloud Infrastructure Service delivery Technical data, compute job information
Legal Authorities Legal compliance Required information for legal requests

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to sensitive data
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, DDoS protection
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion request
  • Compute Job Data: Automatically deleted after job completion
  • Billing Records: 7 years for tax and legal purposes
  • Analytics Data: 2 years in aggregated, anonymized form

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Data portability in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. International Data Transfers

As a global decentralized platform, your data may be processed in various countries. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Binding Corporate Rules for internal transfers

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact our Data Protection Officer at:

Email: privacy@hyperastraai.com
Address: 123 AI Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105

This Cookie Policy explains how HyperAstraAI uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication: Keeping you signed in to your account
  • Security: Protecting against cross-site request forgery
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with our platform

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Platform functionality and security Session or 1 year
Preference Cookies Remembering your settings 1 year
Analytics Cookies Understanding user behavior 2 years

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • Payment Processing: Stripe for transaction processing
  • Customer Support: Intercom for communication

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Delete existing cookies from your browser
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Set your browser to ask for permission before setting cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality and your user experience.
