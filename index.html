Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the HyperlumeAI platform

How does HyperlumeAI ensure the security of my AI models and data?

We use military-grade encryption, secure enclaves, and zero-trust architecture to protect your data. All computations happen in isolated environments, and we never store your raw data after processing.

What types of GPUs are supported on the platform?

We support all major GPU architectures from NVIDIA (RTX series, A100, H100), AMD (Radeon Instinct), and custom AI accelerators. Both consumer and data center grade GPUs are welcome.

How much can I earn by contributing my GPU resources?

Earnings depend on your GPU's capability, availability, and current market demand. High-end GPUs can earn between $5-15 per day during peak demand. We provide a calculator tool to estimate potential earnings.

How does HyperlumeAI compare to traditional cloud providers?

We offer comparable performance at 40-70% lower costs, greater flexibility, and true decentralization. Unlike traditional providers, we don't have capacity limits or vendor lock-in.

What AI frameworks and tools are supported?

We support all major frameworks including TensorFlow, PyTorch, JAX, and Hugging Face. Our platform is compatible with most ML tools and libraries through containerization.
