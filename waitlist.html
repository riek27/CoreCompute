Waitlist FAQs

Common questions about our beta program and early access

When will I get access to the platform?

We're rolling out access in batches starting Q1 2025. Your position on the waitlist determines when you'll get access. We'll notify you via email when it's your turn.

Is there any cost to join the waitlist?

No, joining the waitlist is completely free. When you get access, you'll receive $500 in free credits to get started. There's no obligation to continue using the platform.

What kind of projects is HyperAstraAI best for?

Our platform is ideal for AI training and inference workloads including LLMs, computer vision, reinforcement learning, and any GPU-intensive ML tasks. We support TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Hugging Face.

How much can I save compared to cloud providers?

Early users are reporting 60-80% cost savings compared to AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure for comparable GPU instances. The exact savings depend on your workload and GPU requirements.

Can I use HyperAstraAI for commercial projects?

Yes! Many of our beta users are startups and enterprises building commercial AI products. Your models and data remain completely private and secure.

What support is available during the beta?

Beta users get priority support from our engineering team, access to our private community, and regular office hours with our founders to help with any issues.
